Title: my moon
Author: lojiee

To the moon in the silent sky,
You change through phases, yet never lose your glow ?. 
Sometimes you fade into the darkness,
But you always rise again… brighter than before ?.
You remind me that falling slowly is not failure,
It is only the beginning of rising again ?. 
With patience, with grace,
You return with the same beautiful shine.
Alone in the vast night, you still stand majestic,
Quiet, calm, yet impossible to ignore ?. 
Your glow holds the eyes captive,
Never allowing the heart to look away.
You inspire me without speaking—
Teaching that change is natural,
That even in your smallest phase,
You still carry the promise of fullness ??.
And somewhere between your fading and rising,
You steal my thoughts every night…
Because even the stars know this truth—
The sky looks more beautiful when you are there ???.
